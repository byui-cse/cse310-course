Team Meeting Agenda

You need to meet together as a team at least once this week. Your recommended agenda is as follows:

  1. Opening prayer.

  2. Review the class schedule to remind each other what is happening this week and what is due this week.

  3. Read together as a team the Team Project Description which contains the purpose, requirements, schedule, expectations, and rubric for the project.

  4. Read the Team Contract (see below) as a team and then complete it together.

  5. Begin brainstorming a project proposal. You will submit your project proposal and plan next week.
