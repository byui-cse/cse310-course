CSE 310 | Applied Programming

W05 Teach: Create Team Status Report

  1. Download cse310_team_status_report_campus.

  2. The team leader will need to obtain the following reports from each team member to include in the report:

    • Team Leader - Summary statement of the status of the project and a summary of team communication throughout the Sprint (including any video conference meetings).

    • Scheduler - Summary of tasks completed this Sprint as well as tasks that were deferred to a future Sprint.

    • Graphic Designer - Summary of graphics completed this Sprint (including screenshots).

    • Quality Control - Summary of issues found and fixed during this Sprint. Also a summary of any blocking issues that the team is working on.

    • Configuration manager - Summary of commits made to GitHub along with progress of documentation.

    • Morale Officer - Summary of individual and team successes during the Sprint. Identify struggles and how the team is dealing with them.

  3. The team leader should submit this completed report in I-Learn for the team.
